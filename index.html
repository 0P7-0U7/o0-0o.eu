00 ABOUT

OPT-OUT is a guerrilla engineering collective focused on building radically efficient communication tools for interactive, realtime creative environments.

We prioritize absolute resilience and ultra low latency over fragile, commercial network infrastructures. By embracing a low-tech, bare-metal approach, our libraries bypass bloated routers to give digital artists and performers uncompromised, zero-config stability. It's raw, it's brutal, and the performance is completely bananas.

02 MANIFESTO

Resilience

We bypass fragile commercial network infrastructures. Our tools are designed for zero-config stability in high-stakes, live performance environments where failure is simply not an option.

Low-Tech

We prioritize accessible, cheap, and widely available technology over expensive proprietary hardware. By building for standard $5 microcontrollers, we ensure our high-performance tools can be deployed by anyone, anywhere.

Ultra Low Latency

Microsecond-accurate, real-time data streaming. By eliminating router hops and optimizing telemetry to its absolute minimum, we achieve latency so low it feels instantaneous.

Bananas

Raw, brutal, and completely uncompromising. We push microcontrollers to their absolute limits for creative expression. The performance isn't just fast—it's completely bananas.
